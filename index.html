Initial Contact & Client Hesitation

"Some time ago, I was contacted on Facebook by a player who was representing two other individuals. They were looking for a professional paid Dungeons & Dragons game. They contacted me because I was running a D&D campaign at that time. While we were talking, he explained that they were undecided about joining the group because they used to prefer campaigns with more exploration and combat rather than heavy roleplay."

Case Study: A Negotiation Success

This case study recounts an experience where Mauricio successfully negotiated with new clients, highlighting his flexible approach and focus on mutual benefit. It demonstrates how initial hesitation was transformed into a strong, long-term client relationship.

The Offer and Negotiation

"To address their hesitation, I believed a flexible solution was the best choice in that situation. So, I offered them a 20% discount for the first month if they decided to hire me. From my perspective, this was a reasonable offer, considering they were a group of three clients joining at once. During our negotiation, I set only one condition: all of them had to participate in the free Session Zero together. They agreed, and everything went very smoothly. As a great outcome, the game continued for several months, and they even renewed their subscription for a second campaign, this time paying the full price."

Case Study: A Negotiation Success

Consequences: A Win-Win Outcome

"This situation truly was a win-win for everyone involved. From their perspective, they found the Game Master they were looking for, offering the playstyle they preferred, and they even received a discount for the first month. On my side, I successfully acquired three new clients at once, who later became loyal customers. Initially, they had some doubts about paying for a professional Game Master, but once they experienced the quality of my work, those doubts completely disappeared."

Building Trust & Strong Relationships

"As a result of this positive experience, the trust between us increased significantly. Our professional relationship became much stronger, and they could clearly see that I was the right Game Master for them. Adaptation and Strategic Sacrifice

"This experience taught me the importance of adapting my service when needed, as long as it doesn't compromise my quality or professional integrity. It also showed me that a small sacrifice, like a temporary discount, can lead to significantly larger gains in the future. I believe I made the right decision by offering the discount; if I had insisted on the full price from the start, they might not have joined. Therefore, I will likely use this strategy again if I encounter a similar situation, because it truly works."
